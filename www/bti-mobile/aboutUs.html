Corporate Profile


The Bechtrans Group of Companies (now BTi Logistics), an Australian organization, was established in Melbourne, Australia in 1981, as a privately owned Ocean Freight Forwarder / N.V.O.C.C. In the initial years, Bechtrans concentrated primarily on the U.S. inbound market requirements of its customers and gained a reputation for sound, reliable service in that area. Recognising the need for high quality services from other areas around the world, Bechtrans set in motion a program to expand into other areas of International Forwarding services, at the same time opening offices or appointing agents in all Australian capital cities to facilitate delivery or collection throughout Australia.
